Wet fried Tilapia recipe

Tilapia is an inexpensive, mild white fish thatâ€™s easy to prepare and cook, making it an appealing dinner choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 deep fried tilapia fish
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • Cooking oil (1 table spoon)
  • Crashed garlic (2 cloves)
  • Dhania
  • 1/2 cup of water

Cooking instructions

  1. Put the cooking oil in a pre-heated cooking pan
  2. Add the onions and fry until they turn into a golden-brown color
  3. Add the garlic and cook for half a minute
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook till a paste is formed(about 2 minutes)
  5. Add the water and bring the mixture to boil(or use pre-boiled water).
  6. Dip the fish and cover to cook for about 5 minutes(under low heat)
  7. Sprinkle the dhania on top and set aside
  8. Serve with ugali
